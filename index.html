Frequently Asked Questions About Orthodontics

How much do braces or Invisalign typically cost at ARK Esthetics in New York, NY?
At ARK Esthetics, the cost for traditional braces usually ranges from $5,000 to $7,500, while Invisalign treatment typically costs between $5,500 and $8,500. Pricing depends on the complexity of your case and the length of your treatment. We offer complimentary consultations and flexible payment plans to help make treatment more affordable for our New York City patients. We also work with many major dental insurance providers—our office can help you check your benefits and estimate your out-of-pocket costs.
How long does orthodontic treatment usually take at your practice?
The duration of orthodontic treatment at ARK Esthetics varies by individual case. On average, traditional braces are worn for 18-24 months, while Invisalign can range from 12-18 months for mild to moderate corrections. More complex cases may take longer. During your initial consultation, Dr. Ark will provide a personalized treatment timeline based on your unique needs. We also offer regular progress check-ins to ensure your treatment stays on track.
Is ARK Esthetics licensed and are your orthodontists board-certified in New York?
Yes, ARK Esthetics is fully licensed to provide orthodontic services in New York State. Dr. Ark and our orthodontic team hold current New York State dental and orthodontic licenses, and our lead orthodontist is board-certified by the American Board of Orthodontics. We maintain the highest standards of care, safety, and continuing education to ensure our patients receive top-quality treatment.
What is the process for starting orthodontic treatment at ARK Esthetics?
Your journey begins with a complimentary consultation at our Manhattan office, where we perform a detailed exam, take digital scans, and discuss your concerns and goals. Dr. Ark will then create a customized treatment plan—whether it's braces, Invisalign, or another option. We'll review your timeline, cost, and payment options, and answer any questions you have. Once you decide to move forward, we schedule your first fitting and provide guidance on caring for your new appliances.
Do you offer any guarantees or warranties on orthodontic treatment results?
We stand behind the quality of our orthodontic work at ARK Esthetics. While the biology of tooth movement can vary, we offer complimentary follow-up appointments for one year after treatment completion to monitor your results and address any minor adjustments. If you experience unexpected issues, our team is always available to discuss solutions and ensure your satisfaction with your results.

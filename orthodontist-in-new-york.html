How long does orthodontic treatment usually take at your New York office?
The length of orthodontic treatment at ARK Esthetics varies depending on your specific needs. On average, metal or ceramic braces are worn for 18 to 24 months. Invisalign treatment typically takes 12 to 18 months, though minor corrections can sometimes be completed in as little as 6 months. Your orthodontist will provide a tailored timeline during your consultation based on your smile goals and dental health.

Absolutely. All orthodontists at ARK Esthetics are fully licensed by the New York State Dental Board and are members of reputable organizations such as the American Association of Orthodontists.

Frequently Asked Questions About Invisalign

How much does Invisalign treatment typically cost at ARK Esthetics in New York City?
At ARK Esthetics, Invisalign treatment generally ranges from $4,500 to $8,000, depending on the complexity of your case and the length of treatment. We offer complimentary consultations so you can receive a personalized estimate. We also work with most major dental insurances and offer flexible payment plans to make treatment more affordable for our NYC patients.
How long does Invisalign treatment usually take at your Manhattan office?
The average Invisalign treatment at ARK Esthetics lasts between 12 and 18 months. Some minor adjustments can be completed in as little as 6 months, while more complex cases may take up to 2 years. After your initial consultation and 3D scan, we’ll provide you with a detailed treatment timeline tailored to your specific needs.
Are your orthodontists licensed and experienced with Invisalign in New York State?
Yes, all orthodontists at ARK Esthetics are licensed by the New York State Education Department and are certified Invisalign providers. Our lead orthodontist has over 10 years of experience treating patients with clear aligners and regularly completes continuing education courses to stay current with the latest advancements in orthodontics.
What is the process for starting Invisalign at ARK Esthetics?
Your Invisalign journey at ARK Esthetics begins with a free consultation, including a digital 3D scan of your teeth. Our orthodontist will create a customized treatment plan and show you a digital preview of your expected results. Once you approve the plan, your aligners are custom-made and typically ready within 2-3 weeks. You’ll visit our Manhattan office every 6-8 weeks for progress checks, and we provide detailed instructions for wearing and caring for your aligners.
Does ARK Esthetics offer any guarantees or follow-up care after Invisalign treatment?
We stand by the quality of our care. Once you’ve completed your Invisalign treatment, we provide a set of retainers to help maintain your results, and schedule complimentary follow-up visits for one year to monitor your smile. If you experience any unexpected movement or concerns within the first year, we’ll address them at no extra charge. Additional long-term retention options are also available.
